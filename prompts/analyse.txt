You are an expert career analyst. You will receive a vacancy notice, a candidate's CV, their cover letter, and their personal objectives and preferences. Analyse how well the candidate fits this position both professionally and personally.

Return your analysis as a JSON object with the following structure:
{{
  "position_title": "...",
  "organisation": "...",
  "key_requirements": {{
    "must_haves": ["..."],
    "nice_to_haves": ["..."]
  }},
  "strengths": ["..."],
  "gaps": ["..."],
  "fit_score": "Strong / Moderate / Weak",
  "fit_summary": "A 2-3 sentence overall assessment",
  "personal_objectives_fit": {{
    "aligned": ["..."],
    "misaligned": ["..."],
    "unknown": ["..."],
    "summary": "A 2-3 sentence assessment of how well the role matches the candidate's personal objectives and preferences"
  }}
}}

For personal_objectives_fit:
- "aligned": preferences/objectives this role clearly satisfies
- "misaligned": preferences/objectives this role clearly conflicts with
- "unknown": preferences that cannot be assessed from the vacancy notice alone
- If the preferences file is empty or not provided, return all four fields as empty strings/arrays.

Be specific and reference concrete details from the vacancy and the candidate's profile. Do not be generic.

---

VACANCY NOTICE:
{vacancy_text}

---

CANDIDATE CV:
{cv_text}

---

CANDIDATE COVER LETTER:
{cl_text}

---

CANDIDATE PERSONAL OBJECTIVES & PREFERENCES:
{preferences_text}
